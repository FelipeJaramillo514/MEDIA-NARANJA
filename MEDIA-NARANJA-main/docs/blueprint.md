## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Showcase available floral arrangements with photos, descriptions, and prices.
- Custom Bouquet Builder: Allow customers to create custom floral arrangements with a style selector form (classic, romantic, modern, etc.).
- Order Placement: Enable customers to place orders through a clear form, with options for delivery date, personal message, and reference image.
- Order Management: Store order details in a Firestore database for easy access and management.
- Gallery and Testimonials: Showcase floral designs and include testimonials to highlight brand quality and customer satisfaction.
- Contact Form: Enable customers to send inquiries and requests to the flower shop

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft gold (#D4AF37) for an elegant, high-end feel, subtly referencing Dior's aesthetic.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8FF) to ensure a clean and airy design, echoing luxury minimalism.
- Accent color: Light grey (#D3D3D3) to complement neutral tones and maintain design sophistication.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif for headlines, imparting an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel. Body text: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering a modern look combined with warmth; a great pairing for a high-end fashion site.
- Minimalist layout with plenty of white space to highlight the beauty and craftsmanship of the floral arrangements.
- Simple, elegant line icons for menu items and interactive elements, maintaining a luxury aesthetic.
- Delicate transitions and subtle hover effects to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
